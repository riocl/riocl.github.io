Research Institute Of Odia Children's Literature

ESTD : 1979

Vission & Mission

RIOCL was estalished in 1979 with the following objectives

  • To develop research in children's literature in Odia and other languages in India.
  • To guide Researchers, Critics & Writers of Children's Literature in Odia
  • To organize workshop, conferences, seminar and camp for Researchers, Writer and Critics of Cildren's Literature
  • To organize children's book exhibition and publish books for children.
  • To take correspondence with researchers of India and International field.
  • To give suggestion to Govt. of Odisha and Govt. of India for development of publication of books and magazines
  • To organize project, story-telling camps and competetios in schools
  • To prepare annual publication list of children's books in Odia language
